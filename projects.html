CLASSMATE
Classmate

Classmate is a gamification-based e-learning platform that incorporates game elements into the learning experience. It facilitates interactions between students and teachers, allowing them to access materials, take quizzes, and view rankings. Additionally, Classmate is equipped with text editor and online compiler features to aid in the understanding of programming. The platform utilizes game elements such as leaderboards, levels, experience points, avatars, missions, badges, and more to enhance the learning experience. Furthermore, it has the capability to automatically assess the results of student quizzes.
