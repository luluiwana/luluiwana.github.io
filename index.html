Classmate

Classmate is an innovative e-learning platform that combines gamification elements to create an engaging and interactive learning experience. It includes features such as a text editor and online compiler, built-in assessment tools, leaderboards, levels, experience points, avatars, missions, badges, and more to enhance the student's experience.

STELAR

STELAR is an archival practicum system that helps students understand the administrative process and their roles. It offers a real-world work environment experience. STELAR includes features such as drafting letters, managing mail, tracking dispositions, storing mail, generating letters, automatic signatures, and educational materials and assignments for lecturers to review.

SIDIKTA

SIDIKTA is an android app that enhances student learning through gamification, offering a variety of question types, assignment uploading, and immediate feedback. It also includes a built-in discussion forum for student-teacher and peer communication.
